## What Is Topizzy?

Topizzy makes crypto **practical**.

No banks.  
No mobile money.  
No waiting.  

Just:

1. Connect wallet  
2. Select provider  
3. Enter phone number  
4. Pay with crypto  
5. Receive airtime instantly  

A flat fee of **$0.05** applies per transaction, with **automatic refunds** on any failed top-ups.
